Who We Are

About All About Innovations (AAI)

All About Innovations (AAI) is a registered non-profit society based in North Goa, India. Officially registered on 21 December 2022 under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 (Central Act 21 of 1860) by the Government of Goa, AAI was founded to be a catalyst for positive change — empowering communities, recognising excellence, and making innovation accessible to all.

Our society was established by a founding group of passionate individuals committed to social service and innovation. We operate as a fully transparent, non-profit entity — all income and property is used solely to advance our mission, never distributed among members for personal gain.

AAI operates primarily across the state of Goa, focusing on innovation promotion, social work, community development, and allied public welfare activities.

Our Aims & Objectives

AAI was established with a clear, multi-faceted mission. Here are the core objectives that guide everything we do.

01

Promote Innovation

Foster a culture of innovation, creativity, and forward-thinking solutions across communities in Goa and beyond.

02

Community Development

Drive social work, community development programmes, and service-oriented activities that uplift society.

03

Provide practical assistance and resources to members and the broader community whenever needed.

Associate and cooperate with like-minded organisations sharing similar objectives and values.

Fund Management

Collect, hold, manage, and invest funds responsibly in accordance with all applicable laws.

How We Operate

Organisational Structure

AAI operates through a two-tier governance model — a General Body representing all members, and an Executive Committee handling day-to-day management.

🏛️ General Body

  • All paid-up members with no outstanding dues
  • Elects President, Secretary, Treasurer & two Executive Members
  • Annual General Body Meeting held every October
  • 21 days' notice required for Annual meetings
  • Quorum: 50% of total membership
  • Approves annual report, accounts, and budget
  • Has power to amend the Constitution

⚙️ Executive Committee

  • Consists of President, Secretary, Treasurer & 2 Members
  • 3-year term elected by secret ballot
  • Meets at least 4 times per year
  • Quorum: 4 members
  • Manages daily operations and finances
  • Bank accounts operated by any two of: President, Secretary, Treasurer
  • Can take emergency decisions subject to General Body ratification

💰 Funds & Finance

  • Member registration fees
  • Donations and contributions
  • Government grants and aid
  • Income from investments
  • Fees and collections from events
  • Sale of assets
  • Annual accounts audited by an appointed Auditor

⚖️ Dissolution & Dispute Resolution

  • Dissolution requires 3/5th member vote in a General Body
  • Assets transferred to a similar registered society — never distributed personally
  • Disputes first resolved through mutual discussion
  • Unresolved disputes escalated to President-appointed mediator
  • Final recourse: arbitration by Managing Committee appointee
  • Arbitrator's decision is final and binding
